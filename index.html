HOME

Welcome to the official website of heavy metal band Radarska Kontrola. The band was founded in 2013 consisting of only 3 members. Two acoustic guitars with an electric bass guitar. As the time went by, the band accuired drummer Seno Sejdic who played along Kerim Kraljic, Kerim Dubravic and Emir Avdic. Guitarist Kraljic left the band in 2018, and was replaced by Aldin Djapo. The band plays all around Bosnia and Herzegovina, and are on their way to record an album this year.

BAND MEMBERS

Seno Sejdic

Drummer, 22.
He has been playing drums since he was a kid. Before Radarska Kontrola, him and Aldin had a band called "Siblings" because Seno and his brother played together with Aldin and his siste, hence the name "Siblings". Joined the band in 2016.

Kerim Dubravic

One of the founding members of trash metal band Radaraska Kontrola is Kerim Dubravić. Primarly bass player, occasional guitar player and back vocalist, Kerim is a part of band from the very beggining. Together with his best friend Emir Avdić, he dreamed about playing in a band, but as a guitar player. He was not so good as a lead guitar, so he switched to a bass guitar, just like every single one bassist in the world. That was, as it seems, the best descision for him, since he proved to be good bass player, with a unique style, influeced mostly by Steve Harris of Iron Maiden. Kerim has been in the bend from its beggining, in 2015. He was mostly involved in managing bands gigs, since having a manager in early days of bands existance is not very common thing. He was also responsible for acquiring drummer Seno Sejdić to the band and later, together with Seno, acquiring new guitar player Aldin Đapo. Since Aldin joined the band, carrer went in uprising direction. Kerim is currently, together with other members in the band, working on a bands first album, which is announced for the end of the next year.

Emir Avdic

Emir Avdić was, together with Kerim Dubravić, the founding members of the band Radarska Kontrola. From their earliest age, they dreamed about havin a band and that idea was made true in 2015, when the band Radarska Kontrola started to exist. Emir was always exceptional guitar player and fantastic singer. That is why he stepped up as a singer in the band, while playing ryhtm guitar along the process. He helped band in making a unique style, with high pitched chorus, screams, which are unique for Emir and for which he is recognisible. He is currently working on the bands first album, together with other members of the band. hahah

Aldin Djapo

Aldin came to the band in summer 2018. His heavy sound brought the new dimension to the band. Heavy riffs and fast picking turned this band to a heavy metal band. He went to musical school for piano lessons but ended up playing a guitar. His inspiration comes from Metallica, Slayer, Ghost.
